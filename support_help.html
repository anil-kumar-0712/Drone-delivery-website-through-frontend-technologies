Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place a new order?

You can place a new order directly from your dashboard using the "Order Your First Drone Delivery" button.

How can I track my drone?

Go to the "Track Deliveries" section on your dashboard. You'll see real-time updates there.

What if my delivery is delayed?

We strive for on-time delivery. If there's a delay, you'll be notified immediately. You can also contact our 24/7 support.
